This website aims to assist Chinese language learners in improving their oral reading skills. The text-to-speech feature utilizes the capabilities of your browser. See the note below for more details.

Text to Speech

Select the correct language before clicking "Generate Speech". You may visit zaobao.com.sg and copy and paste some news articles to practice reading aloud.

Note:

This text-to-speech feature utilizes the built-in capabilities of web browsers for speech synthesis.

For the best quality and most natural-sounding speech experience, open this web page using the Edge browser on a Windows PC.

For iOS users, you can select either Tingting(zh-CN) or Meijia (zh-TW) for Chinese text-to-speech synthesis. For Android users, the web browser may not have the necessary capabilities for text-to-speech functionality. Even if it does work, the quality of the speech produced will be very poor.

Paste your Chinese text.
Click the "Generate Pinyin" button to view the Pinyin transcription.
