Text to Speech

Select a language, adjust speed and pitch, then click "Generate Speech". If issues arise, try another language voice.

Note:

Any adjustment to the voice, speed, or pitch requires pressing "Generate Speech" again for it to take effect.

Chinese text-to-speech may not work well on Android devices. However, iOS users have the option to select Tingting (zh-CN) or Meijia (zh-TW) for speech synthesis. On Windows, there are additional options available from Microsoft or Google.
